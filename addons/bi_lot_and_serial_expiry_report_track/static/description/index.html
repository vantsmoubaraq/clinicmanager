Product Lot/Serial Expiry Report Odoo Apps

Track Lot and Serial Expiry Odoo App offers users a seamless solution for generating accurate reports on the expiration status of lots and serial numbers. With this odoo app, users can track the expiry of lots and serial numbers, enabling sufficient product inventory management. A highlight of this app lies in its ability to generate detailed expiry reports in PDF format, allowing users to identify and address any product or serial number nearing expiration. This app feature is valuable for businesses looking to streamline their inventory management processes and ensure timely actions to prevent product losses. Overall, the Lot and Serial Expiry Report Track Odoo App provides user-friendly monitoring and reporting on the expiration status of lots and serial numbers within the inventory system.

Features

Track Lot and serial expiry

User can easily track the expired lot and serial number through report.

Product Wise Stock Expiry Report

User can print stock expiry report based on product.

Lot and Serial Wise Stock Expiry Report

User can print stock expiry report based on lot and serial number.

Print Lot and Serial Expiry Report in PDF

User can print lot and serial number expiry report in PDF format.
